Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly and reliably as possible. Here is what you can expect regarding our shipping services:

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: Delivered within 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivered within 2-3 business days.
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select locations for orders placed before 12 PM.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges vary based on delivery speed, location, and order value. Free standard shipping is available on orders above ₹999.

Order Tracking

After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number to follow your delivery in real-time.

International Shipping

Currently, we ship only within India. International shipping options will be available soon.

Delayed or Lost Shipments

If your package is delayed beyond the expected delivery date or lost, please contact our support team for assistance.
